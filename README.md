<h1> Common Dog Breeds </h1>
<h2> After you learn these breeds, click the link at the bottom to play a review game! </h2>
<img src= "http://qualityaussies.webs.com/Tess%2014%20wks%20tree%20leaves.jpg">
	<p1> Australian Shepherd: These dogs are known for their unique eye colors, often having one green and one blue. They are a medium sized breed grown on ranches and commonly used for herding.</p1>
<img src= "https://pbs.twimg.com/profile_images/803705938125459456/lbICKs79.jpg">
	<p2> Pug: Pugs are a smaller breed, ranging from 14 to 18 pounds. They are known best as companion dogs, great for famlilies and can live for 12-14 years. However, pugs do have some health problems (mostly respiratory) due to their smushed noses.</p2>
<img src= "https://vetstreet.brightspotcdn.com/dims4/default/e23e4d4/2147483647/thumbnail/645x380/quality/90/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fvetstreet-brightspot.s3.amazonaws.com%2F1f%2F92%2Feb11c793404c89e0cab23d9da64a%2FAP-0RHRMQ-ph645080113.jpg">
	<p3> Bulldog: Bulldogs are a breed originated in England. They are a medium-sized dog with a thick-set, low-slung body. Bulldogs are known to adorably snore, drool, and fart because they are Brachycephalic (a condition caused by their short snouts). </p3>
<img src= "http://cdn.akc.org/content/article-body-image/dalmatian_body_image_1.jpg">
	<p4> Dalmation: Dalmations are known as the fire dogs for their help in the firefighting industry. They are identified by their unique black and white spotted coat. </p4>
<img src= "http://www.pawbuzz.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/551/2015/04/smiling-golden-retriever-car.jpg">
	<p5> Golden Retriever: Goldens are a large sized breed. They are bred as gun dogs that would help during hunting season, which is where their name "retriever" originates. </p5>
<img src= "http://cdn2-www.dogtime.com/assets/uploads/gallery/shih-tzu-dog-breed-pictures/shih-tzu-breed-picture-9.jpg">
	<p6> Shih Tzu: These dogs are small in size and are considered a toy dog breed. They weigh only 10-19 pounds, and are thought to have been originated in Tibet and developed in China. </p6>
<img src= "https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcSHDq7IIVbGKFGiYIeOpSnrjRBll9x0I265SFHmaZcpImtTaKwZ">
	<p7> Shiba Inu: These dogs are the smallest breed from China. They are bred for hunting, very agile, and adapt well to mountain environments. </p7>
<img src= "https://gfp-2a3tnpzj.stackpathdns.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/Chiweenie-Puppies-For-Sale-600x600.jpg">
	<p8> Chiweenie:This breed is a Chihuahua-Dachshund mix and are known for their petite size. Altough lovable, they will act as watchdogs and often bark at passing strangers. Typicallly, they live up to 15 years. </p8>
<img src= "
https://static.wixstatic.com/media/7036e3_ec83552c317f4242ba0eda7c1f4c4757.jpg/v1/fill/w_386,h_256,al_c,lg_1,q_80/7036e3_ec83552c317f4242ba0eda7c1f4c4757.webp">
	<p9> Labrador Retriever: Labs are one of the most common family dog breeds. They are intelligent, outgoing, even tempered, agile, gentle, and trusting. Along with the Golden Retriever, they are also known for their hunting and retrieving abilities.</p9>
